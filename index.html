About Me




I'm Farah Adam

NHS Data Sciencist


I'm a data scientist passionate about leveraging data for impactful change. By day, I work as a Data Scientist for the NHS, extracting insights from vast datasets and mastering skills in data analysis, visualization, and machine learning.
I thrive on solving complex problems through end-to-end data science projects, from data collection to delivering actionable insights.
Beyond work, I teach Python as a volunteer and engage in public speaking through Toastmasters International's Data Science Speakers Club.
My education spans from pursuing a Masters degree in Data Science to online courses on platforms like Udemy and Coursera, supplemented by hands-on experience on HackerRank.
Through tutorials on my blog, I share insights and starter code for data science and visualization projects, aiming to support aspiring data scientists on their journey. Join me in exploring these tutorials and resources to dive into the world of data science.
