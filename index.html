About Me




I'm Farah Adam

NHS Data Sciencist


I'm a data scientist driven by the belief that words and data can wield immense power to enact change. By day, I immerse myself in data analysis, and programming, by extracting insights from extensive datasets, honing my skills in data analysis, visualization, and machine learning. I excel in problem-solving, relishing the challenge of end-to-end data science projects, from data collection and cleaning to delivering actionable business insights to clients. After hours, I indulge in my passions, teaching Python programming to children and actively taking part in Public Speaking group as part of Toastmasters International with the Data Science Speakers Club. You are welcome to explore my projects and articles linked below, housed in GitHub repositories for your perusal. My education journey thrives from studying for a Masters degree at a British Universityt to taking online courses from various platforms, where I've absorbed knowledge through courses on edX and Coursera, and hands-on experiences on HackerRank and FreeCodeCamp. In the spirit of giving back, I craft tutorials sharing insights gained along the way, offering starter code for data science and visualization projects. If you're aspiring to venture into the world of data science, I invite you to delve into these tutorials on my blog site.
